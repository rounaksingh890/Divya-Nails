Got Questions?

We've Got Answers

Everything first-timers (and regulars) want to know — answered honestly.

Do nail extensions damage my natural nails?+

Not if done properly and removed safely — which is exactly how we do it. We never force-pull extensions. We soak them off gently, and your natural nails stay healthy underneath. We even apply a strengthening treatment after removal.

How long do gel extensions last?+

With proper care, gel extensions last 3-4 weeks easily. Avoid using your nails as tools (don't open cans with them!), wear gloves while cleaning, and they'll stay perfect. You can come in for infills instead of getting a whole new set.

I've never gotten extensions before. Will it hurt?+

Not at all! There's zero pain involved. You might feel a slight warmth during the curing process under the UV lamp, but that's it. Most of our clients actually find the whole process relaxing. We make sure first-timers feel completely comfortable.

Can I choose any design I see on Instagram?+

Absolutely! Save the pictures, screenshot the reels, and bring them to us. We can recreate most designs you see online. If something isn't possible, we'll honestly tell you and suggest an equally stunning alternative. No false promises.

Do I need to book in advance?+

We recommend booking at least 1-2 days in advance, especially on weekends and during wedding season. Walk-ins are welcome if we have availability, but booking ensures you get your preferred time slot.

What products do you use?+

We use only premium, internationally recognized nail products. Our gels, polishes, and acrylics are all sourced from trusted professional brands. No local-market cheap alternatives. Your nails deserve the best, and that's what they get.
